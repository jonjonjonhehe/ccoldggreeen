Where can we see this Phenomenon?

Here are some of the golden days in some countries where we will vividly and enthusiastically see this aesthetic.

Iceland Times

Iceland's aurora borealis can be seen most frequently between September and April. When the nights are sufficiently dark to observe the aurora. Midwinter, when there are only 4-5 hours of sunshine in Iceland, offers countless opportunity to search for aurora borealis.

Canada Northern Lights

The Northwest Territories, where they are typically visible 240 nights a year, is the best place in Canada, if not the entire world, to view aurora borealis. Fall or winter are the ideal seasons (though summer is pretty good, too).

Northern Lights in Alaska

Despite being more difficult to observe during the Midnight Sun, the northern lights can be seen in all four seasons of the year. Between August and April, when less daylight results in darker night skies, is the greatest time to see the northern lights in Alaska.
